About Us

My Brother’s Keeper isn’t just a name — it’s a promise. We are a family built on love, loyalty, and unity. This began as a dream shared by one of our own — a brother whose heart led us all to give more, love harder, and stand for something greater than ourselves.

We come from all walks of life. Some of us ride, some of us don’t, but all of us live by the same code:
to lift others when they fall, to feed those who hunger, and to bring light into a dark world. We help the homeless, support veterans, and stand beside the families of fallen riders. Every meal, every handout, every kind word — it’s done in his memory.

This group isn’t about titles, ranks, or colors — it’s about heart. We are not a club; we are a bond, unbreakable and proud. We love, we laugh, we fight, and we heal — together. This movement exists because of one man’s dream, and because of the family that refused to let that dream fade away.

Till Valhalla, brother — your spirit rides with us.
Forever united, forever family — My Brother’s Keeper.
