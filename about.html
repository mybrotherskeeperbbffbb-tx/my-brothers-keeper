Our Story • Our Bond

My Brothers Keeper isn’t just a name — it’s a code. Born from loyalty, steel, and the open road, MBK is a brotherhood built on respect, resilience, and realness. We stand shoulder to shoulder through every storm, every victory, and every mile.

We are not defined by colors or clubs — we’re defined by our commitment to each other and the values we ride for. Chrome, grit, and loyalty run through our veins. Every member is a link in a chain that can’t be broken.

Our Mission

To protect, to support, and to uplift our brothers and community. Whether through rides, charity work, or standing by one another — we move as one, for the good of all.

Our Creed

Brotherhood above all. Family before fame. Steel over ego. Once a keeper, always a keeper.

“Ride Together. Stand Together. Live Free.”
