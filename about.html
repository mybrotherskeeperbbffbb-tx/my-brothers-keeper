About My Brother’s Keeper

We are more than just a group — we are a family.
My Brother’s Keeper was born from a bond of love, respect, and loyalty shared among friends who became brothers and sisters. We’ve laughed together, cried together, and stood by one another when life hit the hardest.

This isn’t a club or an organization — it’s a circle of trust. Some of us ride, some of us don’t, but all of us care. Our hearts beat for helping others — from feeding the homeless, supporting fallen riders and their families, to lending a hand where it’s needed most. Whether it’s a food drive, clothing drop, or community outreach, we step up because it’s who we are.

Above all, this movement is dedicated to one of our own — a brother who fell before his time. His spirit, his heart, and his dream live on through us. Every act of kindness, every meal served, every life touched carries a piece of his legacy.

We do this not for fame or praise, but because we understand what it means to love, to lose, and to keep going. This is our promise. This is our way of saying we will always remember, always ride together in spirit, and always keep each other’s backs.

We are My Brother’s Keeper — bound by love, driven by purpose, and united through loss and loyalty.
