About Us

We are more than a group — we are a family. My Brother’s Keeper was born out of love, loyalty, and a promise: to look out for one another and to carry on the dream of our fallen brother.

Some of us ride, some of us don’t — but all of us care. We’re bound by a shared heart for doing good, for helping the homeless, for supporting veterans, for lifting those who have fallen, and for serving our communities in any way we can.

We’re not a club or an organization built on titles. We’re a family bound by respect, trust, and the belief that love and loyalty mean something in a world that often forgets.

Every act of kindness, every meal handed out, every blanket shared, every dollar raised — it’s all for him, and for every soul who’s ever needed a hand when the road got rough.

This page, this movement, this mission — is dedicated to our brother. Gone from our sight, but never from our hearts. Until Valhalla.
