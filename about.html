My Brother’s Keeper

We are not just a group — we are family. Every one of us has shared laughs, struggles, and miles together, and through it all we’ve built something deeper than friendship. This is a bond forged in loyalty, love, and respect — the kind that never fades, no matter the distance or time.

MBK stands for unity and compassion. Yes, some of us ride — but what truly drives us is our hearts. We stand together to feed the hungry, help the homeless, support veterans, and reach out to anyone who needs a hand. We believe in doing what we can, when we can — because sometimes, a small act of kindness is everything.

Above all, this brotherhood was built on love — for each other and for those who can’t stand alone. We’ve laughed together, fought through hard times, and we’ve mourned one of our own. This page, this mission, and this family are dedicated to him — our fallen brother — whose strength, laughter, and spirit will always ride with us. He may be gone from sight, but never from our hearts. Until Valhalla, brother — this one’s for you.
